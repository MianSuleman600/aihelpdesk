Starbit Games - IT Policies & Employee Guidelines
================================================================

1. ACCEPTABLE USE POLICY
================================================================

1.1 Company Resources
- Company computers, email, and network are for business use primarily
- Limited personal use is allowed during breaks
- Personal use must not interfere with job responsibilities
- No streaming video or gaming on company devices during work hours
- Company resources are monitored for security purposes

1.2 Internet Usage
- Do not visit inappropriate or illegal websites
- Do not download unauthorized software (requires IT approval)
- File sharing and torrent sites are strictly prohibited
- Cloud storage must use approved providers (Starbit Box, OneDrive)
- Personal cloud accounts (Google Drive, Dropbox) should not store company data

1.3 Email Usage
- Company email is for business communications
- Do not send confidential information via unencrypted email
- Do not open suspicious attachments or click unknown links
- Report phishing attempts immediately to security@starbitgames.com
- Email signature must follow the approved template
- Auto-forwarding company email to personal accounts is prohibited

2. SOFTWARE AND HARDWARE POLICY
================================================================

2.1 Approved Software
Operating System: Windows 11 Enterprise, macOS 14 Sonoma or later
Web Browsers: Chrome 90+, Firefox 88+, Edge 90+, Safari 14+
Office Suite: Microsoft 365 (company-provided)
Antivirus: CrowdStrike Falcon (mandatory, auto-installed)
VPN: Cisco AnyConnect (for remote access)
Password Manager: Bitwarden (recommended) or 1Password
Communication: Microsoft Teams, Slack (department-specific)
Development Tools: VS Code, IntelliJ IDEA, Git, Docker Desktop

2.2 Software Installation
- All software must be approved by IT before installation
- Self-service software catalog available at https://software.starbitgames.com
- Submit IT ticket for software not in catalog
- Never install software from unofficial sources
- Keep all software updated (automatic updates enabled by default)
- Unauthorized software will be removed by IT without notice

2.3 Hardware Policy
- Company-issued laptops: Dell Latitude 5540 or MacBook Pro M3
- Replacements every 3 years (submit ticket to IT)
- Lost or stolen device: Report immediately to IT and Security
- Remote wipe can be performed on lost devices
- Do not remove company asset tags
- External devices (USB drives) must be company-issued or approved
- Personal devices: BYOD policy requires MDM enrollment

3. DATA CLASSIFICATION AND HANDLING
================================================================

3.1 Data Classification Levels
Public: Press releases, job postings, marketing materials
Internal: Company policies, internal announcements, org charts
Confidential: Employee records, financial data, project plans
Restricted: Trade secrets, source code, customer data, passwords

3.2 Data Handling Rules
Public: No restrictions
Internal: Store in company systems, do not share externally
Confidential: Encrypt at rest and in transit, access on need-to-know basis
Restricted: Highest security, audit logging required, strict access control

3.3 Data Retention
- Employee records: 7 years after termination
- Financial records: 10 years (legal requirement)
- Project documentation: 5 years after project completion
- Support tickets: 3 years after resolution
- Chat history: 1 year
- Email: 2 years (automatic archiving)
- Deleted data: Permanently erased after 30 days in trash

4. INCIDENT RESPONSE PROCEDURE
================================================================

4.1 Security Incidents (report immediately to security@starbitgames.com)
Phishing/Suspicious Email:
  - Do not click any links or open attachments
  - Forward the email to security@starbitgames.com
  - Delete the email from your inbox
  - If you clicked a link, inform IT immediately

Suspected Malware/Virus:
  - Disconnect from network immediately (unplug ethernet or disable WiFi)
  - Do not shut down the computer
  - Call IT Support at ext. 4400
  - IT will run remote diagnostics
  - Do not use the computer until IT confirms it is safe

Data Breach (lost device, unauthorized access):
  - Report to IT and Security within 1 hour
  - IT will remotely lock/wipe the device
  - Security will initiate breach assessment
  - Do not discuss the breach with anyone except IT and Security
  - Follow instructions from the Incident Response Team

4.2 Non-Security Incidents
Hardware Failure:
  - Submit IT ticket with description of issue
  - Loaner device available within 4 hours for critical roles
  - Standard repair: 1-3 business days

Software Crash:
  - Try restarting the application
  - Try restarting your computer
  - Submit ticket with screenshots and error messages
  - IT may remote into your system for diagnosis

5. PASSWORD AND ACCOUNT POLICIES
================================================================

- Password minimum length: 10 characters
- Must contain: uppercase, lowercase, number, special character
- Password expiry: every 90 days
- Cannot reuse last 5 passwords
- Account lockout: 5 failed attempts, 30-minute lockout
- Inactive accounts disabled after 90 days
- Accounts deleted after 180 days of inactivity
- Shared accounts are strictly prohibited
- Each employee must have their own account
- Contractors: accounts expire automatically based on contract end date

6. REMOTE WORK POLICY
================================================================

- Remote work requires manager approval
- Must connect via Starbit VPN for all company systems access
- Workspace must be in a private area (no public WiFi)
- Company laptop must be used (personal devices require MDM)
- Screens must not be visible to others when working with confidential data
- Report any security concerns immediately
- Remote work equipment: company laptop, monitor, keyboard, mouse, headset
- IT provides equipment setup support (submit ticket)
- Internet minimum requirement: 25 Mbps download, 5 Mbps upload
