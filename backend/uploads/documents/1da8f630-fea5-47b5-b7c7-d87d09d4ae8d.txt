Starbit Games - Account & Security Guide
================================================================

1. CREATING AN ACCOUNT
================================================================
- Visit https://portal.starbitgames.com/register
- Use your company email address (@starbitgames.com)
- Choose a strong password (minimum 10 characters, at least 1 uppercase, 1 lowercase, 1 number, 1 special character)
- Verify your email within 24 hours by clicking the confirmation link
- Set up your security questions (these are used for account recovery)
- Complete your profile: full name, department, job title, phone number

2. LOGIN TROUBLESHOOTING
================================================================
Common login errors and solutions:

Error "Invalid credentials":
  - Check that Caps Lock is off (passwords are case-sensitive)
  - Ensure you are using the correct email format (user@starbitgames.com)
  - Try clearing your browser cache and cookies
  - Reset your password using the "Forgot Password" link

Error "Account locked":
  - Account locks after 5 failed login attempts within 15 minutes
  - Lockout duration: 30 minutes (automatic unlock)
  - Contact IT support at it-support@starbitgames.com for manual unlock
  - You can still use "Forgot Password" while locked

Error "Session expired":
  - Sessions timeout after 60 minutes of inactivity
  - Simply log in again to continue
  - Check "Remember Me" to extend session to 7 days

3. PASSWORD RESET PROCESS
================================================================
Step-by-step:
  1. Go to https://portal.starbitgames.com/login
  2. Click "Forgot Password" link below the login button
  3. Enter your registered email address
  4. Check your inbox for a password reset email (sent within 2 minutes)
  5. Click the reset link (expires after 15 minutes for security)
  6. Create a new password (cannot reuse last 5 passwords)
  7. Confirm the new password
  8. You will receive a confirmation email
  9. Log in with your new password

If you don't receive the email:
  - Check your Spam/Junk folder
  - Add no-reply@starbitgames.com to your contacts
  - Try the "Resend Email" option after 5 minutes
  - Contact IT if you still don't receive it after 3 attempts

4. TWO-FACTOR AUTHENTICATION (2FA)
================================================================
2FA is mandatory for all admin, finance, and HR accounts.
Optional for standard user accounts.

Setup process:
  1. Log in to your account
  2. Go to Settings > Security > Enable Two-Factor Authentication
  3. Install Google Authenticator or Microsoft Authenticator on your phone
  4. Scan the QR code displayed on screen
  5. Enter the 6-digit code from your authenticator app
  6. Save your 10 backup codes (stored in a secure location)
  7. Confirm setup by entering another code from the app

If you lose access to your authenticator app:
  - Use one of your 10 backup codes
  - Contact IT support to reset 2FA (identity verification required)
  - Allow 24-48 hours for 2FA reset processing

5. ACCOUNT RECOVERY
================================================================
If you cannot access your account:
  1. Use "Forgot Password" for password reset (works even if locked out)
  2. If you changed your phone number or email without updating your profile:
     - Contact your department manager for verification
     - Manager submits IT support ticket with verification
     - IT will recover the account within 1 business day
  3. For complete account recovery (lost all access):
     - Submit ticket to IT support with subject "Account Recovery"
     - Provide: full name, employee ID, department, manager's name
     - IT will verify your identity via video call
     - Recovery completed within 2 business days

6. SECURITY BEST PRACTICES
================================================================
- Never share your password with anyone (IT will never ask for your password)
- Use a password manager (Starbit recommends Bitwarden or 1Password)
- Change your password every 90 days
- Enable 2FA on all accounts
- Log out when using shared or public computers
- Report suspicious activity to security@starbitgames.com immediately
- Lock your computer when leaving your desk (Windows+L)
- Do not use the same password for work and personal accounts

7. ACCESS LEVELS AND PERMISSIONS
================================================================
Standard User:
  - Access to portal, knowledge base, AI assistant
  - Create and view own support tickets
  - Browse published knowledge base articles

Agent:
  - All Standard User permissions
  - Create and publish knowledge base articles
  - Manage all support tickets (assign, update status, respond)
  - Upload documents for AI training

Admin:
  - All Agent permissions
  - Manage user accounts (create, deactivate, change roles)
  - View analytics dashboard
  - System configuration access
  - Delete knowledge base articles and documents
